Top 5 Movies

1. Avengers: Endgame

The dramatic conclusion to the Infinity Saga, where Earth's greatest heroes come together to reverse the damage done by Thanos.

2. The Lion King

A young lion prince flees his kingdom only to learn the true meaning of responsibility and bravery.

3. Jurassic Park

A park filled with dinosaurs comes alive, and chaos ensues when the creatures break free.

4. Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone

A young wizard embarks on a magical journey at Hogwarts, battling dark forces.

5. Toy Story

A group of toys come alive when their owner is away, experiencing numerous adventures together.
